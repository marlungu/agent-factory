Operations Coordinator — Riverside Community Health

About the role
Riverside Community Health is a mid-sized nonprofit clinic network. We are hiring an Operations Coordinator to keep our day-to-day running smoothly across four clinic sites. You will be the connective tissue between front-desk staff, clinical teams, and leadership.

What you'll do
- Manage scheduling and staffing coverage across four sites
- Track and report on operational metrics (patient wait times, no-show rates, supply levels)
- Coordinate vendor relationships and supply ordering
- Own internal documentation and keep process guides current
- Support onboarding for new administrative staff
- Flag operational risks to leadership before they become problems

What we're looking for
Required:
- 3+ years in an operations, administrative, or coordination role
- Strong written communication and documentation skills
- Comfort with spreadsheets and basic data reporting
- Proven ability to juggle multiple priorities without dropping things
- Experience working directly with frontline staff

Preferred:
- Experience in healthcare, nonprofit, or a multi-site environment
- Familiarity with scheduling software
- A process-improvement mindset

What we offer
- Mission-driven team
- Health benefits and paid time off
- Room to grow into an operations management role

Riverside is an equal opportunity employer.
